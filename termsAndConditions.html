The "Voting for Super Dancer Maharashtra” (“Voting”) will be hosted on SonyLIV app with respect to the program “Super Dancer Maharashtra” (“Show”), telecast on Sony Marathi (“Channel”) for conducting voting for participants (“Contestant(s)”) of the Show who have been selected as the final nine (9) Contestants of the Show by such viewer(s) (“Voter”) who wish to participate in the voting through website and app voting.

Eligibility: Indian residents are only eligible for the purpose of voting for the Show, the employees of Sony Pictures Networks India Private Limited (“Company”) and the producer (“the Producer”) and affiliates, subsidiaries, assignees, representatives and the immediate families of the Company and the Producer as well as the subsidiaries that are sponsoring and/or promoting the Show are not eligible or allowed for voting for the Show.


  1. Medium for voting:

The Voting for Super Dancer Maharashtra will be conducted on the SonyLIV App only.

The SonyLIV App needs to be downloaded from the App store for IOS and Google play for Android. The voter is required to open the App and enter the section for the Show to cast his/her vote for a Contestant by clicking on the photograph/icon of the preferred Contestant. A link to rules & regulations will be available in this App. After the voter submits his / her vote a confirmation message will be displayed on the screen. Voter will be allowed to submit in total 100 (one hundred) votes during the Voting Period (as defined below), unless otherwise stated and subject to the capping norms as defined in the Terms and Conditions herein.


  1. Timelines:


  1. The live voting will commence from November 27, 2018 at 9:00:00 p.m. IST to December 4, 2018 till 8.59:00 p.m. IST (“Voting Period”).

  2. While all efforts will be made to follow the above timelines, however, in the event of circumstances beyond the control of the Producer/Company or for any other reason, these timelines may be altered at the sole discretion of the Company.

  3. Voter is required to be cautious about the valid timelines for the purpose of voting and the eligible Contestants for which he or she intends to vote. The vote(s) received before or after the defined timelines (given above) will not be counted and shall be considered as invalid. The vote(s) sent for Contestants who are not listed under the heading of ‘Top 9 Contestants’ will not be counted and shall be considered invalid.
